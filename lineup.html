JOHN SUMMIT

Headliner

John Summit

John Summit is a Chicago-born DJ and producer who has rapidly gained popularity in the electronic music scene. Known for his infectious house beats and high-energy sets, Summit rose to fame with his breakout hit "Deep End" in 2020, which became a staple in clubs and festivals worldwide. His unique blend of classic house music with contemporary production techniques has earned him a loyal following. In addition to his chart-topping tracks, Summit is recognized for his ability to connect with audiences during live performances, playing at major festivals like Tomorrowland and EDC.

SARA LANDRY

Headliner

Sara Landry

Sara Landry is a rising star in the techno music scene, known for her dark, hypnotic beats and powerful, immersive soundscapes. As a producer, DJ, and sound engineer, she has gained recognition for her ability to fuse industrial techno with raw, emotional intensity. Based in Austin, Texas, Sara is celebrated for her boundary-pushing live sets and has performed at major festivals and underground events worldwide. With a commitment to fostering inclusivity in the electronic music community, she also shares her technical expertise through tutorials and workshops, inspiring the next generation of producers.
